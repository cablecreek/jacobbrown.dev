We are wrestling with "what _is_ ai", and discoving it's capabilities to produce (relativly ok) work. 

You may be thinking, why start a blog if ai could generate it? 

1. Writing can be fun and relaxing
2. I'm hedging my bet that having a resource of ideas available will be important in a world with ai
3. Tracking progress is easier if you write it down

I aim to keep this as my original thoughts but we will have to see how things evolve, whether I keep up the routine of posting regularly, etc. 

For now `init blog`
